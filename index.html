01 — About

Telling stories
that matter

Chilean journalist with over 10 years of experience in strategic communications, academia and science storytelling.

My work focuses on making complex knowledge visible, understandable and meaningful for wider audiences. I have reported on climate change, environmental transitions, and technological innovation in Latin America — from Santiago to Denmark.

I have published in international outlets such as the Danish Development Research Network (DDRN), covering climate and environmental stories from Chile for global readers.

I believe stories can make complex systems visible — and help us imagine more just futures.
